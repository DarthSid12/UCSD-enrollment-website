                        Enrollment Tips & Strategies
                    

                

                
                

                    

                        

                            
                            Best Practices
                        

                        
    
                            
  • 
                                
                                Always have backup courses ready before your enrollment time
                            
    • 
                            
  • 
                                
                                Check prerequisites and time conflicts in advance
                            
    • 
                            
  • 
                                
                                Enroll in high-demand courses first during your window
                            
    • 
                            
  • 
                                
                                Use multiple devices/browsers for critical course enrollment
                            
    • 
                        

                    

                    
                    

                        

                            
                            What to Watch Out For
                        

                        
    
                            
  • 
                                
                                Don't rely solely on waitlists for required courses
                            
    • 
                            
  • 
                                
                                Avoid enrolling too close to the add/drop deadline
                            
    • 
                            
  • 
                                
                                Don't forget to check for course time conflicts
                            
    • 
                            
  • 
                                
                                Don't wait until the last minute to plan your schedule
